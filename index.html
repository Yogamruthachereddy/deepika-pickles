The Story of Deepika Pickles

Our journey began in Chittoor, Andhra Pradesh, inside a warm family kitchen filled with the aroma of roasting mustard seeds, fenugreek, and cold-pressed oils. Deepika Pickles was born out of a passion for preserving traditional Indian culinary heritage, using age-old recipes passed down through generations.

We believe that pickles are not just side dishes, but the heart of an Indian meal. That is why we source the freshest local mangoes, plump lemons, and green roselle (Gongura) leaves directly from local farmers. Every single jar is packed by hand under strict hygienic standards, ensuring an authentic taste that reminds you of home.

Why Choose Us

We strive to bring the purity and taste of home to your dining table. Here is what makes our pickles special.

100% Homemade

Every single batch of Deepika Pickles is prepared by hand in small kitchen quantities, rather than mass-produced in heavy factories.

Traditional Andhra Taste

Passed down from grandmother recipes, our pickling methods retain the vintage aroma and taste that has defined South Indian kitchens for centuries.

Fresh Ingredients

We use premium local quality mangoes, fresh garden limes, high-grade ground spices, and pure cold-pressed oils for pickling.

No Artificial Preservatives

Our pickles are naturally preserved by acidity, salt, and oil content. We add zero artificial food colors, chemicals, or stabilizers.

Hygienic Preparation

Cleanliness is our top priority. We use sterilized jars, dry utensils, hair coverings, and gloves during preparation and packaging.

Premium Packaging

Our jars are sealed with double leak-proof packaging to lock in freshness and guarantee spill-free delivery to your doorstep.

Fast Delivery

We pack and ship our pickles immediately after receiving orders. Delivery reaches across India with prompt transit tracking.

How To Order

Ordering our fresh, homemade pickles via WhatsApp is extremely simple. Follow these three quick steps.

1

Choose Your Pickle

Browse our catalog of Veg and Non-Veg pickles. Select your package weight (e.g. 500g, 1kg) and quantity directly on the card.

2

Click Order Button

Click the "Order" button on the card. You will be redirected to WhatsApp with a pre-filled message detailing your selection.

3

Confirm & Pay

We will calculate the shipping, share payment details (UPI/GPay/PhonePe), double-seal your jars, and ship them to your address.
